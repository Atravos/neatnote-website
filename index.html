Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers.

Is NeatNote really free?

Yes, NeatNote is completely free to download and use. We believe in providing a high-quality note-taking experience without any paywalls. If you find the app useful, we welcome donations to support ongoing development.

Where are my notes stored?

All your notes are stored locally on your device. NeatNote does not use cloud storage, ensuring your data remains private and accessible even without an internet connection.

How do I back up my notes?

NeatNote stores all your notes in a folder within your user directory. You can back up this folder manually or use your preferred backup solution. In future updates, we plan to add automatic backup functionality.

Can I sync my notes across devices?

Currently, NeatNote does not include built-in sync functionality. However, you can use third-party services like Dropbox or Google Drive by placing your notes folder within your synced directory.

What file formats does NeatNote support?

NeatNote currently works with plain text (.txt) files. Support for Markdown and other formats is planned for future updates.
